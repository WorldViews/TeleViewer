How to Use TeleViewer

Viewing YouTube Videos

The left side menu contains categories of YouTube videos. By selecting a category the world will populate with icons based on the category. Each icon is geolocated based on data supplied by YouTube, Each icon will be the location of a video related to the selected category.  After clicking an icon the selected video will play in a window over the globe. The video window can be moved around the screen for easier viewing or to allow for selecting another video.

Blog and Wiki Entries

We also have categories in the left menu related to Wiki entries and personal blogs. When a wiki or blog category is selected the globe will populate with entry icons. Clicking an icon will bring up a window with the selected entry displayed.

Tours and Hikes

When the Tours and Hikes category is selected the world will be populated with icons that look like the earth. Clicking on the icon will bring the user to the location of a tour on the map.  Clicking again on the tour path will start a 360 degree YouTube video of that path.

World Navigation

Mouse Navigation:

Pan View - To pan the view with a mouse or trackpad left click and drag.

Zoom View - To zoom the view right click and drag or use the scroll wheel.

Rotate View - To rotate the view Middle click + drag, or

CTRL + Left/Right click + drag.

Touchscreen Navigation:

Pan view - Using one finger press down on screen and drag.

Zoom view - Using two fingers press down on screen and pinch fingers toward each other.

Tilt view - using two fingers press down on screen and drag in the same direction.

Rotate view - Using two fingers press down on screen and rotate hand.
